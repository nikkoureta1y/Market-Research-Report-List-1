<p><p>The servo brakes market is divided into three main types: Vacuum Servo Brakes, Pneumatic Servo Brakes, and Hydraulic Servo Brakes. Vacuum Servo Brakes use engine vacuum to amplify brake force, offering effective stopping power in light vehicles. Pneumatic Servo Brakes utilize compressed air, often used in heavy-duty vehicles for reliable performance. Hydraulic Servo Brakes operate using hydraulic fluid, providing precise control and responsiveness, making them suitable for a wide range of vehicles. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance needs and vehicle design.</p></p>
