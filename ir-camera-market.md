<p><p>The IR camera market consists of various materials, each with unique properties suited for specific applications. Sapphire is robust and has excellent optical clarity, suitable for high-temperature environments. Germanium provides good performance in the 2-14 micrometer range, commonly used in telecommunications and military applications. Silicon, while primarily effective in the near-infrared range, is cost-effective for commercial infrared imaging systems and sensors.</p></p>
