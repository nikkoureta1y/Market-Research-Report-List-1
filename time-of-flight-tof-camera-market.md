<p><strong>The Time of Flight (ToF) Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Time-of-flight Camera</li><li>CCD Time-of-flight Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) cameras are categorized primarily into two types based on their sensor technology: CMOS and CCD. CMOS Time-of-Flight cameras utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor sensors, offering advantages like faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integrated circuitry, making them ideal for applications in mobile devices and robotics. CCD Time-of-Flight cameras, on the other hand, use charge-coupled device sensors, known for their high image quality and sensitivity but at a higher cost and power consumption, suitable for professional and industrial applications.</p></p>
