<p><p>The Long-range Radar Transmitting and Receiving Module market features two main types: BiCMOS and RFCMOS. BiCMOS (Bipolar CMOS) technology combines the strengths of bipolar and CMOS transistors, providing high speed and improved performance, making it suitable for applications requiring robust signal processing. In contrast, RFCMOS (Radio Frequency CMOS) integrates RF functionality with CMOS technology, offering lower power consumption and enhanced integration. Both technologies are pivotal in advancing radar systems for applications such as automotive, aerospace, and defense.</p></p>
