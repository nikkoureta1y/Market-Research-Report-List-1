<p><p>Industrial X-ray computed tomography (CT) scanners are categorized into stable and movable market types based on their design and application. Stable scanners are typically installed in fixed locations, providing high precision for detailed inspections in manufacturing and quality control. They are ideal for large components and require more space. In contrast, movable scanners are portable, allowing for on-site inspections and flexibility in various settings. These are suitable for smaller components and facilitate ease of use in different operational environments.</p></p>
