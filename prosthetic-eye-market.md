<p><p>The prosthetic eye market is primarily categorized into non-integrated implants and integrated implants. Non-integrated implants are traditional prosthetic solutions that sit externally and do not connect with the body's biological systems. In contrast, integrated implants involve advanced technologies that merge with the ocular structure, enhancing functionality and aesthetic appeal. Integrated options often offer better visual outcomes and may include electronic components, while non-integrated implants focus on the cosmetic aspect, providing a more natural appearance for individuals with eye loss.</p></p>
