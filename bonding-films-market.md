<p><ul><li>Thermally Cured</li><li>Pressure Cured</li><li>Others (Chemically Cured and Light cured)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bonding Films market encompasses various types based on curing methods. Thermally cured films require heat to initiate the curing process, providing strong adhesion suited for high-performance applications. Pressure cured films cure under applied pressure, offering efficient bonding without the need for additional heat. Others include chemically cured films, which utilize chemical reactions for bonding, and light-cured films that harden upon exposure to specific light wavelengths. Each type is tailored for specific industrial needs, enhancing versatility in applications.</p></p>
