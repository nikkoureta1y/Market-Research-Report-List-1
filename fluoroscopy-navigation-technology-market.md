<p><p>Fluoroscopy navigation technology encompasses two primary market types: 2D and 3D fluoroscopy systems. The 2D fluoroscopy system utilizes real-time imaging to provide two-dimensional visualizations, primarily aiding in procedures that require quick decision-making and immediate feedback. In contrast, the 3D fluoroscopy system offers enhanced spatial orientation by providing three-dimensional imaging, which improves accuracy in complex interventions. Both systems are integral in various medical fields, including orthopedics, cardiology, and surgical navigation, enhancing procedural outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
