<p><p>The companion animal pain management market is divided into two primary types: external use and internal use. External use products, such as topical analgesics, ointments, and patches, are applied directly to the skin to alleviate localized pain. Internal use products include oral medications, injections, and intravenous treatments that target pain systemically, providing relief from conditions affecting deeper tissues or organs. Both types aim to enhance the comfort and quality of life for pets suffering from pain or discomfort.</p></p>
