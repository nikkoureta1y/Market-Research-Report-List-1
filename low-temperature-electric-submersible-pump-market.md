<p><p>The Low Temperature Electric Submersible Pump market includes two primary types: AC pumps and DC pumps. AC pumps, commonly used in larger applications, offer high efficiency and are suited for continuous operation in colder environments. In contrast, DC pumps are more versatile, often found in smaller applications where energy efficiency and variable speed control are essential. Both types cater to industries such as oil and gas, mining, and wastewater management, addressing specific needs for low-temperature pumping solutions.</p></p>
