<p><ul><li>Fluid Warmers</li><li>Convection Warming Systems</li><li>Conduction Warming Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veterinary temperature management equipment comprises several key types designed to maintain animal body temperature during medical procedures. Fluid warmers are used to heat IV fluids and blood products to prevent hypothermia. Convection warming systems utilize circulating warm air to raise the temperature of animals effectively. Conduction warming systems involve direct contact with heated surfaces or blankets, transferring warmth to the animal's body. Each type serves to enhance patient recovery and comfort in veterinary settings, ensuring optimal thermal regulation.</p></p>
