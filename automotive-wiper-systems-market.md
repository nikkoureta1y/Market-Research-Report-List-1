<p><ul><li>Bone Wiper</li><li>Boneless Wiper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wiper systems market primarily consists of two types: bone wipers and boneless wipers. Bone wipers feature a metallic or plastic framework that supports the wiper blade, providing durability and stability during operation. In contrast, boneless wipers rely on a streamlined design without a traditional frame, offering a sleeker appearance and improved aerodynamic performance. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with bone wipers often favored for their robustness and boneless wipers for their modern aesthetics and light weight.</p></p>
