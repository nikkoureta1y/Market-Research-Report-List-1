<p><ul><li>2-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li><li>1-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine coolant temperature sensor market features two main types: 2-wire and 1-wire coolant temperature sensors. </p><p>2-wire coolant temperature sensors use two wires for power supply and signal transmission, providing accurate readings of coolant temperature. They are commonly utilized in modern vehicles, ensuring efficient engine performance and preventing overheating. </p><p>Conversely, 1-wire coolant temperature sensors simplify installation with just one wire, sending temperature data directly to the engine control unit. Their compact design makes them suitable for specific applications where space is limited.</p></p>
