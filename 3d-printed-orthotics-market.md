<p><p>The 3D printed orthotics market encompasses three primary types: upper-limb orthoses, lower-limb orthoses, and spinal orthoses. Upper-limb orthoses support arms and hands, enhancing functionality for conditions like fractures or paralysis. Lower-limb orthoses, designed for the legs and feet, aid in mobility for injuries or deformities. Spinal orthoses provide support to the spine, offering relief from conditions such as scoliosis or back pain. These customized solutions improve comfort, fit, and patient outcomes through advanced 3D printing technology.</p></p>
