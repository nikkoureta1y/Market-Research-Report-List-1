<p><p>The automotive airbags market encompasses various types designed for passenger safety. Frontal airbags deploy during head-on collisions to protect the driver and front passenger. Side airbags provide protection in side-impact crashes, cushioning the torso. Curtain airbags deploy from the roof to shield occupants from side impacts and rollover incidents. Knee airbags safeguard the lower limbs during frontal collisions. Other airbags may include pedestrian airbags and seatbelt airbags. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and reducing injury risk.</p></p>
