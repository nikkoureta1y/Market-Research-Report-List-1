<p><strong>The Laboratory Hematology Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sheath Flow Hematology Analyzers</li><li>Laser Hematology Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory hematology analyzers are essential for blood analysis and diagnosis. Sheath flow hematology analyzers utilize a fluid sheath to concentrate blood cells, allowing for precise measurement and differentiation of cell types. They are widely used for routine blood counts. In contrast, laser hematology analyzers employ laser technology to analyze cells by measuring light scattering as cells pass through a laser beam, providing detailed information on cell morphology and subpopulations. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in clinical laboratories.</p></p>
