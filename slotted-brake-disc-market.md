Ventilated slotted brake discs feature internal air channels that enhance cooling, reducing brake fade during intense use. Perforated brake discs incorporate holes to improve ventilation and water dissipation, enhancing wet-weather performance. Slotted brake discs have grooves that help release gas and debris, optimizing braking efficiency.
