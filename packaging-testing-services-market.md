<p><p>The Packaging Testing Services market encompasses three primary types: physical, chemical, and microbiological testing. Physical testing evaluates the packaging's durability, strength, and resistance to environmental factors such as temperature and moisture. Chemical testing assesses the interactions between packaging materials and their contents, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations. Microbiological testing focuses on detecting microbial contamination, crucial for food and pharmaceutical packaging. Together, these testing types ensure that packaging meets safety, quality, and performance standards, safeguarding products and consumers alike.</p></p>
