<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acid-resistant adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that are ready for application, offering convenience and ease of use. They cure through a chemical reaction with moisture or heat. In contrast, two-component adhesives require mixing a resin and a hardener before application, providing enhanced performance and chemical resistance. This type is often preferred for demanding environments where durability and strength are critical, particularly in industrial and construction applications.</p></p>
