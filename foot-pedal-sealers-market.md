<p><p>Foot pedal sealers are specialized sealing machines used to package various products. The market is divided into two main types: one-sided and double-sided sealers. One-sided foot pedal sealers apply heat to only one side of the packaging material, making them ideal for simple sealing tasks. In contrast, double-sided sealers apply heat to both sides simultaneously, providing stronger seals and enhanced durability, suitable for more demanding packaging applications. Each type caters to different sealing needs in various industries.</p></p>
