<p><p>The Freight Forwarding Services Market encompasses various shipping methods tailored to cargo needs. Full Container Load (FCL) involves dedicating an entire shipping container to a single client's goods, providing cost efficiency for large volumes. Less-than Container Load (LCL) combines shipments from multiple clients, allowing businesses to share container space, which is ideal for smaller shipments. Other market types may include air freight, road, and rail transportation, catering to diverse logistical demands and ensuring flexible solutions for shippers.</p></p>
