<p><ul><li>Mono-tube</li><li>Twin-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway shock absorbers market primarily features two types: mono-tube and twin-tube designs. Mono-tube shock absorbers consist of a single tube containing both the fluid and gas, providing a more responsive and consistent performance under varying loads. Conversely, twin-tube shock absorbers have an inner tube encased within an outer tube, allowing for greater fluid capacity and stability. Both types are essential for enhancing ride comfort and control in railway systems, contributing significantly to vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
