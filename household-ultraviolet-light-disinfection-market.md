<p><strong>The Household Ultraviolet Light Disinfection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary cathode low pressure mercury UV disinfection</li><li>High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection</li><li>Ozone UV disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household ultraviolet light disinfection market consists of three main types. Ordinary cathode low-pressure mercury UV disinfection uses low-energy mercury vapor to generate UV light, effectively killing bacteria and viruses. High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection employs powerful UV-C lamps for rapid and extensive disinfection, suitable for larger spaces. Ozone UV disinfection combines UV light with ozone to enhance microbial eradication and eliminate odors, providing a dual-action approach to air and water purification in homes.</p></p>
